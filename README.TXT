Smart Prescriptions

Project Flow

1) Patient reaches Doctor for medication or treatment, and registers using email, phone number.
2) Doctor after consulting the patient and identifying the medical problem, creates a smart prescription.
3) Only Doctors/Authorized medical practitioners have perimission to create a smart prescription.
4) The patient receives the smart prescription via email, sms, whatapp.
5) The patient takes the smart prescription to the dispensary and validates the prescription at the dispensary.
6) The pharmacist completes the smart prescription and delivers the medicines/drugs to the respective patients.
7) Thereby prescribed medicines reach the intended patient.
